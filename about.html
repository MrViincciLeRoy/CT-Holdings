About CT Holdings

A registered South African financial services firm built to help small and medium businesses stay compliant, organised and financially healthy.

Who We Are

Reliable. Affordable. Compliant.

CT Holdings and Investments (Pty) Ltd was founded in March 2025 with a clear purpose: give South African small and medium businesses access to professional financial services that are accurate, timely and genuinely affordable.

We understand the South African regulatory landscape — SARS deadlines, CIPC obligations, payroll compliance — and we help our clients navigate it without stress. Whether you're a sole trader needing basic bookkeeping or a growing business with employees and VAT obligations, we tailor our service to your stage and budget.

Our clients get direct access to the directors — no call centres, no passing you between departments. Just hands-on, personal service from people who know your business.
