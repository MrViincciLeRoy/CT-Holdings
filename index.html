Reliable. Affordable. Compliant.

CT Holdings & Investments was founded to give South African small and medium businesses access to professional financial services that are accurate, timely and affordable. We understand the regulatory landscape and help our clients stay compliant with SARS, the Companies Act and CIPC requirements.
