SARS Submissions — VAT Returns

VAT returns prepared, reconciled and submitted to SARS accurately and on time. Standard returns from R1,200.

VAT Compliance

Never Miss a SARS Deadline Again

VAT is one of the most common compliance headaches for South African businesses. Late or incorrect submissions lead to SARS penalties and interest — both of which are avoidable with the right support.

We prepare your VAT return from your records, reconcile it thoroughly, and submit directly to SARS via eFiling. You'll receive confirmation of submission and a copy for your records.

VAT submissions are included in our Core SME and SME + Payroll bookkeeping packages, or can be handled as a standalone service.
