Tax Compliance

Stress-Free Annual Tax Submissions

Every individual and business registered with SARS is required to submit an annual tax return. Filing late, incorrectly or not at all attracts penalties and can trigger an audit.

We prepare your return from your financial records, ensure all deductions and income are correctly declared, and submit via SARS eFiling. We handle individuals, sole traders and registered businesses of all complexities.

Whether it's a simple ITR12 or a complex business return with multiple income streams, we make sure it's done right.
