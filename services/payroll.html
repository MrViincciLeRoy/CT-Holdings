Payroll & PAYE Services

Monthly payroll processing, payslips and PAYE submissions — so your employees are paid correctly and SARS is kept happy.

Payroll Management

Accurate Payroll. Compliant PAYE. Every Month.

Payroll is one of the most time-consuming and compliance-sensitive tasks for any business with employees. Getting it wrong — incorrect PAYE calculations, late EMP201 submissions, or errors on IRP5s — can lead to SARS penalties and unhappy staff.

We process your payroll monthly, issue payslips to each employee, and submit your EMP201 to SARS on time. At year-end we handle your EMP501 reconciliation and issue IRP5/IT3(a) certificates.

Payroll is included in our SME + Payroll bookkeeping package or can be quoted separately based on your number of employees.
